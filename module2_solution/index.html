Samosa

A fried or baked pastry with a savory filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese or lentils ( rarely beef and other meats, in case of a non-vegetarian samosa filling). It may take different forms, including triangular, conical (most common), or half-moon shapes. Best served either w/ spicy mint or sweet tomato chutney.
